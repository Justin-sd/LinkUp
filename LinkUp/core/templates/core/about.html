About Us

Who Are We?


We are a group of students from University of California San Diego (UCSD) that got together in CSE 110. We founded the idea from the struggles we had organizing a meeting time with a large group of ten members. We created LinkUp as a way to ease the creation of meetings with even the busiest of schedules.
